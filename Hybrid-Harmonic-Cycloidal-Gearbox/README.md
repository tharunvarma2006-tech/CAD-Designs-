# Hybrid Harmonic-Cycloidal Gearbox

## Overview
Developed a novel gearbox combining harmonic and cycloidal drive principles with a reduction ratio of 30:1 for use in a 5-DoF robotic manipulator.

## My Contributions
- Designed the transmission architecture and major components.
- Developed a rolling-ball transmission mechanism to replace the conventional flex-spline.
- Manufactured and tested multiple design iterations.

## Key Specifications
- Reduction Ratio: 30:1
- Application: 5-DoF Robotic Manipulator
- Payload Capacity: 7 kg
- Gripper Capacity: 2 kg

## Results
- Successfully integrated into the manipulator.
- Improved reliability through rolling contact transmission.
- Validated through prototype testing.
